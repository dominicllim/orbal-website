FAQs

How does on-device AI work?
On iPhone 15 Pro and newer, Orbal processes your notes entirely on your device using Apple's built-in models. Your notes never leave your phone. Older iPhones use secure cloud processing — your notes are never stored or shared.

Is my data backed up?
Orbal syncs across your devices via iCloud. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and only accessible on your Apple devices.

How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual notes or contacts at any time from within the app. To remove all data, simply delete the app.
